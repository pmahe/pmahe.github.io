--- layout: home title: Pierre Mahé subtitle: Research engineer - machine learning and computational biology --- Welcome to my homepage !

I work at bioMérieux within the Data Science department, and I am also a part-time associate professor at the University of Grenoble Alpes.

I have a broad interest in applying machine learning algorithms to biological data. Over the last decade, I have mainly be involved in clinical microbiology applications, like identifying a bacteria or predicting its antibiotic resistance profile from various technologies (e.g., mass-spectrometry, imaging and next-generation sequencing). I am currently particularly interested in analyzing next-generation sequencing data using k-mer based and/or deep-learning approaches.

On this website, you can find information about the courses I teach and a link to my publications.
